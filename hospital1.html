The Stressed State of Hospital Administration

A UI/UX case study revealing how outdated systems impact patient care in small to mid-sized Indian hospitals.

The Patient Experience in Numbers

Survey data from 13 respondents highlights the systemic failures and their direct impact on patient well-being and confidence.

How Stressful Was Your Visit?

A majority of patients experience significant stress, not from their medical conditions, but from the administrative hurdles they face during their visit.

Rating of File & Record Organization

Over two-thirds of patients rate the hospital's record-keeping as poor or very poor, a root cause of treatment delays and repetitive questioning.

Willingness to Adopt a Digital System

Despite the challenges, patients are overwhelmingly positive about embracing a digital solution for booking and accessing their records.

Repeating Medical History

Lost files and poor inter-departmental communication force a significant number of patients to recount their entire medical history multiple times in a single visit.

38.5%

Recount their history 4 or more times per visit.
